Welcome to Sparrow Haven

Sparrow Haven, your bush home away from home. Sparrow haven is a charming and warming retreat where relaxation meets adventure and nature!

Here at Sparrow Haven we offer a 2 bedroom house and a 1 bedroom flat on the property making it an amazing experience for couples, families or friends looking for an amazing and unforgettable relaxing bush experience.



The House:

The main house comes equip with everything you need. the 2 bedrooms come with a queen bed and a double bed to accommodate for your needs, 3 bathrooms, aircons and fans to help keep you cool and comfy in the warm summers, a fully equip kitchen ready to for you to prepare all your favorite meals, a braai area perfect to capture the evening skies and to sit around and socialize, a pool and jacuzzi to unwind and enjoy the outdoors and the nature that surrounds in both the summer and winter, a lounge equip with a smart TV and board games where you can get all nice and cozyand enjoy a some fun family time or put on a movie and get comfy and cozy, and wifi to stay connected and share your amazing holiday at your bush home away from home.

The Flat:

The flat comes packed with 1 bedroom consisting of 2 single beds to accommodate for 2 people, 1 bathroom, aircons and fans to be cool and comfy in the summer time, the braai area to enjoy cooking outdoors, a fully equip kitchen for your meal preparation, a smart TV to enjoy your all your show and movie favorites, a cold water jacuzzi to relaxing in and board games to bond with friends, family or even by yourself.
