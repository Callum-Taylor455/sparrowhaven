Welcome to Sparrow Haven

Sparrow Haven, your bush home away from home. Sparrow haven is a charming and warming retreat where relaxation meets adventure and nature!

Here at Sparrow Haven we offer a 2 bedroom house and a 1 bedroom flat on the property making it an amazing experience for couples, families or friends looking for an amazing and unforgettable relaxing bush experience.



The House:

  • 2 Bedrooms: A queen bed and a double bed to accommodate your needs.
  • 3 Bathrooms: Plenty of space for everyone to get ready.
  • Aircons & fans: Stay cool and comfortable in the warm weather.
  • kitchen: Fully equipped to prepare your favorite meals.
  • Braai Area: Perfect for outdoor cooking and socializing.
  • Pool and jacuzzi: Relax and unwind in our pool and cold water jacuzzi
  • Lounge: Cozy up and enjoy our smart TV and board games.
  • WI-FI: Stay connected with our reliable internet.

The Flat:

  • 1 Bedrooms: Two single beds to accommodate up to 2 people.
  • 1 Bathrooms: Conveniently located for your needs.
  • Aircons & fans: Stay cool and comfortable.
  • kitchen: Fully equipped for meal preparation
  • Braai Area: Enjoy outdoor cooking and relaxation.
  • Smart TV: Enjoy your favorite shows and movies.
  • Jacuzzi: Relax in our cold water jacuzzi.
  • Board Games: Have fun and bond with family and friends, or solo.
